Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account (name, email address, password) and the expense records you log while using Spendly, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve the Spendly service — to display your expense history, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and protected with industry-standard measures. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. No personal or financial data is shared with any third-party service. We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
